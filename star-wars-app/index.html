Trivia Game Instructions

Before Starting Game

Before starting the game, there are a few very important things to be aware of while playing.

1. In order to play the game, you must select a difficulty.
2. When answering a question, you must ONLY click the "lock in answer" button ONCE! This is IMPORTANT! DO NOT CLICK the button more than once.
3. Once you lock in an answer, the question will disappear. This is to inform you when its time to go to the next question. You can do so by clicking the "Next Question" button. Only click once.
4. When the game is over, you will be given a game over screen with your results. You can view the status of your game with a helpful question counter for your benefit.
5. Enjoy the Game and have fun!
